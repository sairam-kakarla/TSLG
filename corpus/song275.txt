<p><span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS">Naa manasukemayindi nee maayalo padindi

nijama kalaa thelisedelaa

naaku alaage undi enno anaalanundi

dachedela lolopalaa

mana iddariki theliyandi yedo jarige untundi

anduke inthala gunde uliki paduthu undi

Naa manasukemayyindi nee maayalo padindi

nijama kalaa thelisedelaa</span></p>
<p>Chukkale thecchi ivvanaa andi nee meeda nakunna prema

kottaga undi bothi ga nammalenantha ee vintha dheema

jantaga venta nuvvunte andada naaku naa chandamaama

anduke naaku nuvvante maatalo cheppalenanta prema

panchukunna muddulo ilaa jate padi

penchukunna mathu lo padi mate chedi

gaalito cheppani mana modati gelupu idani

Naa manasukemayyindi nee maayalo padindi

nijama kalaa thelisedelaa</p>
<p>Eppudu gunde chappudu kottu kuntundi nee peru laaga

eppudo appudappudu gurthu kosthundi naa peru koddiga

ontiga undanivvadu kallalo unna nee roopu rekha

inthaga nannu evvaru kammukoledu neelaa ilaaga

lokamante iddare ade manam ani

swargam ante ikkade ante sare ani

vennele paadani mana chilipi chelimi kadhani </p></div
